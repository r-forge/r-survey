Specifying certainty PSUs

In many national surveys there will be major cities that are sampled with certainty (eg New York) and then subsampled. In addition to the usual work-arounds such as treating the second-stage units as PSUs, the survey package has two ways to specify certainty PSUs directly.

The best way to specify certainty PSUs is to use a finite population correction and specify the first-stage population size as 1 for that stratum.
